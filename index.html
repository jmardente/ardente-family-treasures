Behind the stories

About Ardente Books and Gifts

Ardente Books and Gifts was created to share meaningful children's stories and thoughtful gifts that spark imagination. Through Cora's adventures, young readers are invited to explore the ocean, celebrate friendship, and appreciate the natural world.

Every story is written with warmth, curiosity, and the belief that a good book can stay in a child's heart long after the final page.
